How to identify original Rudraksha?

Prefer clear photos from multiple angles, natural mukhi lines (not carved), and lab testing for high-value beads.

Which product is best for beginners?

Many beginners start with 5 Mukhi Rudraksha or a Tulsi mala for a simple, consistent routine.

How to care for mala, bracelets and crystals?

Keep them clean and dry, avoid harsh chemicals and water exposure, and store respectfully when not in use.
