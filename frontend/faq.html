Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about authenticity, usage, shipping and care.

Rudraksha

How do I identify original Rudraksha?

Look for natural mukhi lines (not carved), clear multi-angle photos, and for costly beads consider reputable lab testing.

Which Rudraksha is best for beginners?

Many beginners start with 5 Mukhi for comfortable daily wear and a simple routine.

Mala & Bracelets

How should I care for Tulsi mala or Rudraksha bracelet?

Keep it clean and dry, avoid harsh chemicals and water exposure, and store respectfully when not in use.

Gem Stones

Do natural crystals look exactly like photos?

Natural stones can vary in shade and banding patterns—this variation is normal.
